COOKBOOK

We are a group of passionate computer science students from Batangas State University - Alangilan. Together, we form a team of five individuals, each bringing a unique set of skills and creativity to the table.

This project is the culmination of our efforts for the finals, where we explored our love for both coding and cooking. Inspired by the fusion of technology and culinary arts, we embarked on a journey to create a platform that celebrates the art of cooking with a touch of innovation.

Our goal is to provide a delightful and seamless experience for food enthusiasts. We've carefully curated a collection of recipes that showcase the richness of flavors, all accompanied by mouth-watering photos that capture the essence of our culinary creations.

As a reminder, this project is intended for educational purposes and should not be used without our explicit notice. It is not meant for production online, and we appreciate your respect for our hard work and dedication in bringing this culinary endeavor to life.
